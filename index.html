I am currently an associate professor at Institute of Applied Mathematics, Academy of Mathematics and Systems Science, Chinese Academy of Sciences in Beijing, China.

My research is mainly focused on the partial differential equations in fluid dynamics, especially for the nonlinear stability of wave phenomena in gas dynamics.
